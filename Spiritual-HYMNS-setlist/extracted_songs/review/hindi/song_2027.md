# Song 2027

**NUMBER:** 2027
**TITLE:** Chottey bachho ko paas aane do
**LANGUAGE:** Hindi
**BOOK:** special_hindi
**KEY:** G

## SECTIONS

### Verse 1
Chote bachon ko paas aane do,
Aashish unhen bhi pane do
Unhen mana mat karo mere paas aane do
Swarg ka rajya hai aison ka। (२)

### Verse 2
Nanha balak aaya Yeshu paas,
Apna khana laya Yeshu paas
Aashish maagi Prabhu Yeshu ne,
Khaya paanch hajar logon ne। (२)

### Verse 3
Dena seekhen apne Prabhu ko,
Apna sab kuch de den usi ko
Dena lene se dhanya hai,
Yeshu ka yah anmol vachan hai। (२)
